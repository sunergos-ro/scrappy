# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Accept and give feedback professionally.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private maintainer contact issue titled `CODE_OF_CONDUCT: private report` without sensitive details.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
